A little something about myself

I'm a self-taught product and web designer from Romania. I enjoy working on product design, design systems, and everything that involves no code tools such as Webflow, Wix, Squarespace.

I'm a lifelong watcher of american football on both the collegiate and national level, everyday learner and inconsistent reader. At the moment I am based in the beautiful region of Alsace, in eastern France.

To get in touch with me regardless of subject just, drop me a line.

Design should be simple

I don’t have a formal background in design. This may sound alarming, but I consider a generalist approach an important asset.

I studied Electronics & Telecommunications at ETTI, Cluj-Napoca, before working as a designer in various startups where the medium involved learning fast and wearing a lot of hats which made the work more challenging and fun.

I think product design isn't as complicated or as difficult as everyone pretends it is. I believe good design should be simple and clear with a pragmatic focus on business growth.
